WORKING

Steps to Get Started

  • 1. Land to any section using our navigation menu.
  • 2. To scan, click on the SCAN button in Home Section.
  • 3. Once the doc is scanned, it will be available in PDF format.
  • 4. To use any of the tools, for eg: JPG to PDF, PDF to Word etc click on their respective buttons.
  • 5. A dialog box will pop-up asking for you to choose the files, choose the files from here.
  • 6. Now select the destination to which you want your converted doc to be saved.
